About Us

This android application for the safety of persons, and this app can be activated by a single click, whenever need arises

A single click on this app identifies the location of place through GPS and send a message comprising this location URL to the registered contacts and also alerts the nearest police station help the one in dangerous situations.

The unique feature of this application is to send the message to the registered contacts continuously for the every one minutes until the "STOP" button of the application is clicked.

Continous location tracking information via SMS helps to find the location of the victim quickly and can be rescued safely.
